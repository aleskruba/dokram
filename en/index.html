The company DOKRAM s.r.o. was established on April 22, 2015 as a direct successor to the activities of PLASTOCHEM BRNO spol. s.r.o., transport and forwarding division.
We are a forwarding company specializing in the provision of services in the field of road freight transport.

We provide these services in road transport

  • Domestic and international full truck transports
  • Domestic and international loading transports
  • Transportation of goods in ADR mode
  • Oversized shipments

As part of forwarding services, we have the following vehicles at our disposal

  • classic semi-trailers – 90 m³ with a tonnage of up to 25 t
  • large-volume trucks – 110-120 m³ with a tonnage of up to 24 t
  • sola (22 m³- 50 m³ s tonáží 1,9t - 10 t)
  • vans, box or tarpaulin
